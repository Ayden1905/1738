Cafes and resturants in Australia

An overview of cafes and resturants in Australia

Two seperate visualisations showing the how seating capacity changes per suburb, as a percentage and number

This map showcases the locations of cafes,resturants and takeaway locations in Inner City Melbourne alongside the tram lines to showcase their location relative to the trams and how their distance varies.

This Bubble chart shows how through the years, the number of cafes changed.

This line chart showcases cafes/resturants against takeaway, showing how cafes retain their gap in locations and continue to climb, wherea takeaways never increase by a large margin, and stay consistent.
